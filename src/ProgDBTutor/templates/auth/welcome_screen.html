Game Aspects Explanation

Welcome to the game aspects explanation page. Here you can learn about various settings and features available in the game to enhance your experience.


Farm
  • Building: left-click to drag the building, right mouse button for info and to upgrade

Settings
  • Log-out: Click the log-out button to securely log out of your account.
  • Sound: Adjust the game volume using the sound buttons. You can increase or decrease the volume as per your preference.
  • Mute: Use the mute button to quickly mute all game sounds.
  • Background Sound: Toggle the background music on or off to suit your liking.
  • Zoom: Adjust the zoom level of the game interface to get a better view or more detailed perspective.

Market
  • Picture: The figures indicate the type of crop
  • Price: Under the figure you can see the price of 1 piece of the corresponding crop type
  • Amount: You can adjust it with the plus button and minus button, or directly enter the amount yourself by entering it in the number between the plus and minus button
  • Sell: Click the sell button to sell all selected crops

Chat
  • Chat: You can select a friend to chat with in the chat box
  • Search: You can search another player and add them as your friend
  • Visit: You can also select a friend and visit them via the "visit world" button

Leaderboard
  • Here you compare yourself to the best players in the world

Attack
  • Steal: Steal as many crops from your opponent as possible
  • Opponent: Better players bring in more, but are harder to beat
