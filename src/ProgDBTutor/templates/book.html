Game Overview

Welcome to the game overview page. Here you can learn about various features and settings available in the game to enhance your experience.


Farm
  • Buildings:
    Left-click a building if you want to move it. Right-click a building to see more information and to check out its stats, or upgrade it.
  • Farming:
    Right-click an empty field to see what crops are available. Select the crop you like, and now it's time toooo wait... Watch the crops grow as you play the game and when a field's edge becomes green it's time to harvest. Just click a field to harvest the crops, after which they will be stored in your silo.
    Quickly check out your resources with the resource button in the top-left. Upgrading fields will give you acces to more crops which you can sell for higher prices on the market. Each subsequent crop you unlock will take twice as long to grow as the previous crop.

Market
  • The market is the place to be if you want to sell your crops and get those well-earned coins. Hold the '+' button or type in a number to specify the amount of crops you want to sell.
  • The price of each crop is dynamically decided by how many players are selling at the moment. So check out the prices regularly!

Attack
  • Steal: Steal as many crops from your opponent as possible.
  • Opponent: Better players bring in more, but are harder to beat.

Exploration
  • Time: You can select a time for which you go exploring with or without animals
  • Animals: You can select a few animals (if you have them) to go explore, the total amount of animals is limited by the Bay building level
  • Risk: Longer exploration, bring more risk with them. More risk means, animals might not return
  • Post-exploration: Open your rewards and claim the surviving animals

Chat
  • Chat: You can select a friend to chat with in the chat box.
  • Search: You can search another player and add them as your friend.
  • Visit: You can also select a friend and visit them via the "visit world" button.

Leaderboard
  • Here you compare yourself to the best players in the world.

Settings
  • Log-out: Click the log-out button to securely log out of your account.
  • Sound: Adjust the game volume using the sound buttons. You can increase or decrease the volume as per your preference.
  • Mute: Use the mute button to quickly mute all game sounds.
  • Background Sound: Toggle the background music on or off to suit your liking.
  • Zoom: Adjust the zoom level of the game interface to get a better view or more detailed perspective.
