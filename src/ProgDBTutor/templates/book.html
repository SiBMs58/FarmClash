Game Aspects Explanation

Welcome to the game aspects explanation page. Here you can learn about various settings and features available in the game to enhance your experience.

  • Log-out: Click the log-out button to securely log out of your account.
  • Sound: Adjust the game volume using the sound buttons. You can increase or decrease the volume as per your preference.
  • Mute: Use the mute button to quickly mute all game sounds.
  • Background Sound: Toggle the background music on or off to suit your liking.
  • Zoom: Adjust the zoom level of the game interface to get a better view or more detailed perspective.
