Farm Clash

Your farm is the most important place
in the game. this is where you plant crops,
buildings upgradeand much more

1. ranking: here you can compare yourself with your friends
and the best players in the world
2. this button makes your resources visible
3. The number of coins you have
4. button to go to the chat
5. button to go to the settings
6. button to go to the market
7. button to go to the attack menu

- With a click with the left mouse button on a building,
you can move it, and then with another click on the left mouse button,
you can place the building.
- Clicking on a building with the right mouse button takes you to the building's
information menu where you will find information about the building, the level
of the building and much more.
- To see other parts of your farm you can drag the mouse
